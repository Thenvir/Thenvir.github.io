Proud to be
Bangladeshi

I am a first-generation immigrant from Bangladesh since 2006. I am also proud to be a first-generation college student. With academics and and a large amount of help, I've managed to succeed and find my passion in Web Development. Nothing excites me as much as creating a product to showcase and be used by others. On this site you'll find several things I've worked on as well as a blog I update bi-weekly.

My future goals include being part of a team that creates meaningful products, and giving back to my community. With the latter, I refer to my Bangladeshi community in Paterson as well as back home. In my first country, I dream to create a non-profit Tech school in order to advance the level of education and fight poverty head on.
